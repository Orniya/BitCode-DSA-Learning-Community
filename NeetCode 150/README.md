## 📌 Overview

After completing the **DSA for Beginners course** and building a solid foundation through topic-based practice, we now move into the **NeetCode 150 challenge** — a carefully curated set of interview-level problems designed to sharpen real-world problem-solving skills.

This phase is where concepts turn into instinct.

We focus on:
- strengthening algorithmic thinking  
- recognizing patterns under pressure  
- improving coding speed with accuracy  
- developing interview-ready problem-solving fluency  

👉 The goal is not just to solve problems, but to understand the structure behind every solution.

---

## 🎯 Approach

- Solve **1 problem per day** with full focus  
- Prioritize deep understanding over speed  
- Identify the underlying pattern in every question  
- Revisit problems until solutions feel natural, not forced  
- Track progress consistently and refine thinking over time
